Absolutely. Our suites have multiple outlets near beds, spacious layouts for medical equipment, and wheelchair accessibility. We can also arrange for additional furniture removal if needed for medical devices. Please let us know your specific requirements when booking.

We understand medical schedules are unpredictable. We offer flexible check-in/check-out times and can often accommodate stay extensions or early departures. Our cancellation policy is more lenient for medical circumstances - we're here to support you, not add stress.

Yes, our ground-floor units are wheelchair accessible with roll-in showers. We can provide additional assistance with luggage, transportation recommendations, and connecting you with local medical supply companies. Our staff is trained to assist guests with mobility challenges.
